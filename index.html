Welcome to the website of the AIA research group of the UCLouvain, led by Yves Deville, Pierre Dupont, Sébastien Jodogne, Siegfried Nijssen, Pierre Schaus and Hélène Verhaeghe. Our research focuses on the algorithmic aspects of artificial intelligence. Topics we study are:

  • Constraint programming
  • Constraint satisfaction and optimization
  • Data mining
  • Pattern mining
  • Explainable artificial intelligence
  • Exploratory data analysis
  • Machine learning
  • Deep learning
  • Data visualisation
  • Health informatics and bioinformatics
  • Medical imaging
